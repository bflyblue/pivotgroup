We are level 1 BBBEE

Pivot painting and renovation solutions is a level 1 BBBEE company that is owner managed. We strive to provide a professional hands-on approach in order to exceed our stakeholders expectations. We specialize in the painting, refurbishment and restoration of buildings.

Our highly skilled teams do all types of industrial, protective and decorative painting, specialized coatings, floor coatings, facade cleaning and refurbishment, joint sealants, concrete rehabilitation, tenant installation, demolition and now also specialize in
IEFS (External Insulation Finishing System).

Our work is generally carried out in occupied buildings and in the public eye, so it is imperative that we always operate with the highest possible standards of quality and safety.

Our History

With a proud history of 37 years, Pivot Painting and Renovation Solutions (PTY) Ltd is a leading painting, renovation and building refurbishment company, operating in all major centers within South Africa.

In March 2017 a successful deal was concluded whereby Tri-Star Painting and Renovation, together with its management structures and combined experience of over 45 years formed a level 1 BBBEE company, now known as Pivot Painting and Renovation Solutions.

Our long standing reputation of consistently delivering projects on time, whilst ensuring quality and safety, remains our focus and commitment to all our clients.

Introducing our top Management

SELLO MASILO - COMMERCIAL DIRECTOR

Mr Masilo is a BSc QS honours and Advanced Management graduate from Witwatersrand University. After graduating in 2005 he started his working career at Group 5 Building as a Quantity Surveyor. Sello started working with Tri-Star Painting & Renovations in 2008.

His keen work ethic and skills in both commercial and quantity surveying sectors saw him promoted to be the first EE director in the Tri-Star Group of Companies.

In March 2017 Sello was part of the management team that purchased Tri-Star Painting and Renovations and now is trading as Pivot Painting and Renovation Solutions a Level 1 BBBEE company.

SHAUN MEGANNON - MANAGING DIRECTOR

Has a successful track record in the Paint and Renovations Industry spanning over 30 years.

Shaun has established himself as a respected and seasoned business man during this time. He worked for some of the largest Painting & Renovations and Construction companies as well as running his own company for several years before joining Tri-Star Painting & Renovations in 2002.

Together with Sello they concluded a successful purchase of Tri-Star Painting and Renovations and he remains the MD of Pivot Painting and Renovation Solutions.
